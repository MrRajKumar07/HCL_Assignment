School Management System

### Classes for Teachers, Students, and Courses

---

## 1️. One-Liner Elaboration (Business Understanding)

A *School Management System* models:

* Students enrolling in courses
* Teachers teaching courses
* Course schedules and assignments


---

## 2️. Functional Scope

### Core Features

* Student registration
* Teacher assignment
* Course creation
* Enrollment management
* Course listings per user
