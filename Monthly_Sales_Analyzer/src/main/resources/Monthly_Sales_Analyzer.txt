Analyze Monthly Sales from a CSV

program that reads sales data from a CSV file and analyzes month-wise 
sales performance.
The program should compute key metrics such as total monthly revenue, 
average sales, and identify the highest and lowest performing months.
This task simulates a real-world business reporting scenario 
where raw transactional data is converted into actionable insights.


Read structured sales data from a CSV file
Process and aggregate data on a monthly basis
Generate summary metrics useful for decision-making
Present results in a clear and readable format
