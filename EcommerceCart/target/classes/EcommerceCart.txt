E-commerce Cart

### Objects for Products, Users, and the Shopping Cart

---

## 1️. One-Liner Elaboration (Business Understanding)

An *E-commerce Cart* system allows users to:

* Browse products
* Add/remove products to a cart
* Maintain quantities
* View cart totals
* Prepare data for checkout (pricing, tax, discounts)

This is the *heart of any e-commerce backend*.

---

## 2️. Functional Scope

### Core Features

* Product catalog
* User management
* Shopping cart per user
* Quantity handling
* Price calculation

### Non-Functional

* Data consistency
* Easy extension (offers, coupons)
* Clean separation of concerns
